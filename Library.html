Library Management Software

This Library Software as the name implies is a digital tool that is installable on a desktop computer and can run on all versions of windows. It is designed to track books moving in and out of a library and taking note of there destinations

Why our Library Management Software

  • User friendly and no requirement for basic education to operate
  • It organizes books by categories
  • An option for easy book search by names, category, author and publisher/date
  • It creates a profile for book borrowers
  • It keeps records of books issued to borrowers
  • It generates a report for each books issued to a borrower
  • It keeps record of borrowers history
  • It generates an amount to be paid as penalty for defaulters
  • It provides data on books conditions in real time
  • It throws notifications on books not returned after due date
  • List of books borrowed and returned can be retrieved between dates
  • It helps u carry out a survey or analysis on the books in demand
  • It will track the most borrowed books. This will help you know the books on high demands
  • It will track the least borrowed items. This will help you know the books on low demands
  • It will track dormant books. This will help you know the unpopular books
