Business Management and Accounting Software(Retail and WholeSale)

This Point of sale Software is a digital tool that is installable on a desktop computer and can run on all versions of windows. It manages and organises stock levels, sales, record keeping and tracking of all day to day transactions carried out in a retail and wholesale shop

Why our Business Management Software

  • User friendly and no requirement for basic education to operate
  • Checking of too much stock and too little stock
  • It provides data on stock conditions in real time
  • It organises your warehouse and help track items moved in and out of it
  • It generates receipt for customers
  • It tracks sold products, time of sales, profits made
  • It will help you order stock in the right quantities at the right time so that you will never over or under stock
  • It will track the most sold items. This will help you know the products on high demands
  • It will track the least sold items. This will help you know the products on low demands
  • It will track dormant items. This will help you know the stock to stop purchasing
  • Cost saving since it helps cut expenses by minimizing the amount of unnecessary products in storage

Admin Page Module consists of:

Admin Dashboard from which all settings are done

This Module is only accessed by the Admin. Here, he will be able to monitor daily, weekly, monthly, quarterly, bi-annual and yearly sales

Other records like total stock, new stock, finished stock, about to expire stock, expired stock, low stock is viewed at a glance

Transactions are summarized by day, week, months and year

Transaction Analysis is carried out to determine the rate of sales

Inventory Module:

Stock taking is done here

Quantity of items decreases on sales

Notifications alerts are set to determine low and finished stock

Unit Sales and purchase prices are recorded so as to help calculate gains and losses

Sales Form Module:

Receipts are generated on each sales

Quantity of items decreases from inventory on each sales

Discounts, Vats are calculated if required

Unit Prices are multiplied by quantity to give the accurate Total prices

WareHouse Module:

Items in the warehouse is recorded with this module

Incoming and out going supplies are recorded here

Returned and damaged goods can be noted here

Suppliers details can be recorded
